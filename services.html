Tribeca

Step in, step back and get your rocks off in our New York inspired bar and pizzeria. Their selection of unique handmade pizzas cater for every taste and their huge choice of beers from around the globe wash them down nicely.

If cocktails are more your tipple then chill out and indulge in a few retro classics followed by some tantalizingly tasty modern creations. And if you're into more active pleasures, they have live Premiership football every week throughout the season and dj sets that will get your mojo back into the groove.

Budget Rating ££

Bakchich

Bakchich is quite simply a little piece of Lebanon that sits on the chic and quirky Bold Street in Liverpool. This most beautiful and lovingly crafted restaurant is, from start to finish an experience and flavour of the Mediterranean.

Budget Rating £

Crust

A brand spanking new restaurant, bar and dynamic space in Liverpool’s upcoming Bold Street zone. Pushing the boundaries with their creative take on true Italian classics, they offer a range of different options for your dining pleasure. Sit back and relax with a hot slice of pizza and a cold beer at Crust.

Budget Rating ££

Mowgli

Mowgli is all about how Indians eat at home and on their streets. She was born to feed the raw need Indians have for tiffins full of fresh bright intense flavour. Mowgli is not about the intimate, hushed dining experience. It is about the smash and grab zing of healthy, light, virtuosic herbs and spices.

Budget Rating £
